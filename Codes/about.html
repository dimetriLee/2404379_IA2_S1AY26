About Face Off

Our Story

Face Off was founded in 2025 by a passionate lacrosse player who saw a need for high-quality, affordable lacrosse equipment. What started as a small online shop has grown into one of the premier destinations for lacrosse gear in North America.

We believe that every player, from beginners to professionals, deserves access to top-tier equipment that enhances their game. Our team consists of former players, coaches, and enthusiasts who understand what it takes to excel on the field.

Our Mission

To provide players of all levels with premium lacrosse equipment while delivering exceptional customer service and fostering a love for the sport.

Our Values

Quality First

We only stock equipment from trusted manufacturers that meet our rigorous quality standards.

Customer Focus

Your satisfaction is our priority. We're here to help you find the perfect gear for your needs.

Community

We support local lacrosse programs and initiatives that grow the sport we love.

Innovation

We stay ahead of trends and bring you the latest advancements in lacrosse technology.
